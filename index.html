Privacy Policy for Cinedairy

Last Updated: 21/11/2025

Cinedairy (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a mobile application that allows users to track movies they have watched in theatres. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Cinedairy app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information (via Clerk)

Cinedairy uses Clerk to securely handle account creation, login, and authentication. Clerk may collect:

  • Email address (required)
  • Email OTP verification data (required)
  • First Name (optional)
  • Last Name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • User ID (auto-generated)

1.2 User-Generated Content

You may enter or upload the following inside the app:

  • Movies you watched
  • Watch dates
  • Ratings
  • Notes or comments
  • Optional posters or images

1.3 Automatically Collected Data

This includes device information, app version, crash reports, and basic anonymous analytics. None of this is linked to your identity unless required for debugging.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Verify your email using OTP
  • Store your watch history and ratings
  • Sync your profile across devices
  • Improve app performance
  • Provide customer support

We do NOT sell your data, share it with advertisers, or track you outside the app.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

Your data is stored securely using Clerk and our backend database. All communication uses encrypted HTTPS connections.

4. Optional Profile Information

You may optionally upload a profile picture and provide your first and last name.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except for essential service providers like Clerk. We never share data for advertising.

6. Children’s Privacy

Cinedairy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Data Retention

Your data remains stored as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, all associated data is permanently removed.

8. Data Deletion / Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing: vitsmithun01@gmail.com

We process deletion requests within 48 hours.

9. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Update your data
  • Delete your data
  • Stop using the app anytime

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. When changes are made, the new version will include an updated “Last Updated” date.
